What is TableForge?

A focused hobby app for Warhammer 40k players who want to track progress, keep armies organised, record battles, and stay motivated through the hobby journey.

Track units

Keep on top of what is boxed, built, painted, based, and tabletop ready.

Build armies

Group your collection into armies and see points and composition at a glance.

Log battles

Record games over time and build a usable history of your tabletop play.

Share progress

Post updates, photos, and milestones from your hobby journey.

Stay connected

Follow friends, join private groups, and keep momentum going between games.

About us

Why we are building TableForge.

Built by hobbyists

TableForge is being built by two hobbyists who wanted a better way to track painting progress, organise armies, record games, and stay motivated in the hobby.

Our goal is to create a home for hobbyists who want to move models from sprue to tabletop and enjoy the journey along the way.
