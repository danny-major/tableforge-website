About us

Why we are building TableForge.

Built by hobbyists

TableForge is being built by two hobbyists from the UK who wanted a better way to track painting progress, organise armies, record games, and stay motivated in the hobby.

Our goal is to create a home for hobbyists who want to move models from sprue to tabletop and enjoy the journey along the way.
